# A Curated List of Remote-First Companies Hiring in Europe
Finding your next remote job in Europe can be a challenge. While many companies are embracing remote work, it's not always easy to find a comprehensive list of those that are actively hiring. This repository is a community-driven effort to create and maintain a curated list of companies that offer remote-first and fully remote positions across Europe.

Whether you are a software engineer, a designer, a product manager, or in any other field, this list is intended to be a valuable resource for your job search. The goal is to make it easier for talented professionals like you to connect with forward-thinking companies that prioritize a flexible work environment.

### Contributing
This is a living list and your contributions are welcome! If you know of a company that hires remotely in Europe and is not on this list, please feel free to open a pull request. Together, we can build the most comprehensive resource for remote job seekers in Europe.
